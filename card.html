Lions do most of their hunting at night as their eyes have adapted to the dark and gives them a huge advantage. Which makes them nocturnal hunting animals.

      Average Length:Females: 63-72 in Males: 72-82in

Lions have strong, compact bodies and powerful forelegs, teeth and jaws for pulling down & killing prey. They rip through their prey's tough skin, ligaments and crush bones with their massive fanged tooth deadly claws.
